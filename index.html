Elixirs of the Void

Libations for the long night ahead

The Nameless Fog
Signature cocktail of the evening
  • 2 oz blue curaçao
  • 1 oz white rum
  • ½ oz lime juice
  • Top with lychee juice
  • Dry ice (optional, use safely)
Pale, glowing, and unsettling. Suitable.
Shoggoth's Blood
For the truly unafraid
  • 2 oz dark rum
  • 1 oz blackcurrant liqueur
  • ½ oz grenadine
  • Splash of cola
  • Black sugar rim
Disturbingly viscous. Best not to ask what's in it.
The Innsmouth Look
Non-alcoholic option
  • Blue lemonade
  • Cucumber slices
  • Mint and basil
  • Splash of tonic water
  • 1 drop green food colouring
Refreshing, faintly oceanic. You'll fit right in.
The Miskatonic Mule
Academic and devastating
  • 2 oz vodka
  • ½ oz elderflower liqueur
  • Ginger beer to top
  • Squeeze of lemon
  • A single black olive
The olive is the singular forbidden truth at the bottom of the cup.
Pickman's Model
Not for the weak of stomach
  • 1.5 oz mezcal (smoky)
  • ¾ oz Aperol
  • ½ oz blood orange juice
  • Charcoal salt rim
Looks wrong and tastes like something ancient and beautiful.
Elder Sign Seltzer
For the designated keeper of bodies
  • Sparkling water
  • Elderflower cordial
  • Cucumber and mint
  • A thin slice of lemon
Pure, protected. Your investigators need you.
